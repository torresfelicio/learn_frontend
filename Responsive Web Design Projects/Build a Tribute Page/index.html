GODS OF METAL

A tribute to the brothers from one of heavy metals finest bands

Damebag Darrell and Vinnie Paul were the driving force behind one of Heavy Metals greatest bands - Pantera.

Guitarist Dimebag Darrell and brother Vinnie Paul on drums started Pantera in 1981. The band eventually became the key band in the genre of Power Groove Metal, going on to inspire Metal bands for generations to come.

Dimebag started playing guitar aged 12, at aged 14 he won a guitar contest in Dallas, Texas. The prize for the competition was a new Dean ML guitar, a brand that Dimebag would go on to play for the rest of his career. Dimebag also entered the same competition a few months laterand won again, forcing the sponsors of the competition to ask him not to enter again to give other kids a chance at winning.

Dimebag and Vinnie Pauls dad was Jerry Abbott, a country music songwriter and producer from Abilene, Texas.

The brother initially started out as a glam metal outfit in the 80's before recruiting Phil Anselmo in 1986 and eventually transforing their sound into a more extreme and heavier sound. The brothers tight interlocking rhythms and grooves became their trademark style, and to this day are instantly recognisable.

Dimebag Darrell was shot and killed on-stage during a concert in 2004 while his brother, Vinnie Paul was playing drums. Vinnie Paul passed away in his sleep due to a heart attack in 2018.
